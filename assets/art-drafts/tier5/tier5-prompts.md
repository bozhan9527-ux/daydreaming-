# 五階職業角色美術 AI 繪圖提示詞

反推自現有 1~4 階角色圖(`assets/sprites/hero/jobs/`~`jobs4/`)的畫風:精細像素風角色立繪,
白底方便去背裁切,半身到全身、chibi比例(頭偏大但不到Q版誇張),角色手持/穿戴對應職業的
標誌性道具服裝,構圖乾淨適合當角色預覽圖。跟彩蛋插圖(anime插畫風)是不同風格,不要混淆。

## 圖片尺寸

跟既有 jobs2~jobs4 資料夾一致,直式全身/半身構圖即可,單張生成建議 512x768 或類似直式
比例,白底透明化方便後製去背。

## 通用風格前綴(每張生成時都要加在提示詞最前面)

```
Detailed pixel art character portrait, chibi-adjusted proportions (slightly
oversized head, natural adult body), same young man protagonist across all jobs:
short messy dark navy-blue hair, tired half-lidded eyes, neutral calm expression.
Clean pixel art with visible pixelation and crisp cel-shaded coloring (not flat
retro 8-bit), muted low-saturation Morandi color palette with small warm accent
colors (deep red, gold) on key details. Plain white background for easy cutout,
front-facing or slight 3/4 angle standing pose, holding or wearing job-specific
signature prop, portrait/full-body game character illustration style.
```
